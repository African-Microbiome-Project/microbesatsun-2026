## Applications

Applications for **Microbes at SUN 2026** are open to postgraduate students (MSc and PhD) and postdoctoral researchers. Priority will be given to applicants based at African institutions.

**Space is limited** — early application is encouraged.

---

## What to Prepare

Your application should include:

- A brief motivation statement (max 300 words) describing your research background and why you want to attend
- Your current CV
- Confirmation of your institutional affiliation
